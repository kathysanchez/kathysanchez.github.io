ABOUT

I'm a researcher and data analyst in criminal justice and econ. My interests include government data infrastructure, transparency, violent crime, weapons, economic mobility, occupational regulation and administrative regulation. Much of my research has focused on seizures and forfeitures, and my work has been cited by Justice Gorsuch, ProPublica and other outlets.

Currently, I'm at the Council on Criminal Justice where I manage research contracts, analyze data and write reports. Before the Council, I worked at Maryland's sentencing commission and the Institute for Justice.

I live in Baltimore, MD. Things I love include working out at my home gym, heavy metal, making yummy, nutritious food, playing with my pit bull mix and various hobby projects like making this website with ChatGPT.
