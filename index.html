ABOUT

I'm a researcher and data analyst in criminal justice and econ. I work at the Council on Criminal Justice where I manage research contracts and support its commissions by analyzing and presenting data. Before the Council, I worked for Maryland's sentencing commission and at the Institute for Justice.

My interests include government data systems, transparency, weapons, economic mobility, occupational regulation and administrative regulation. Much of my research has focused on seizures and forfeitures, and my work has been cited by Justice Gorsuch, ProPublica and other outlets.

I live in Baltimore, MD. Things I love include playing with my pit bull mix, fitness, heavy metal, a great bargain like hosting this website on GitHub for "free" and various hobby projects like making this site with ChatGPT.
